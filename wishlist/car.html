YES! It's could be Ford Ranger Raptor


The 2021 Ford Ranger Raptor is meant to head off-road. As with European models, this off-road spec Ford Ranger will contain a beefier and sturdier off-road suspension, Terrain Management System, and a robust chassis. The new Ford Ranger Raptor features a higher ground clearance and larger ride height than the standard Ranger trucks. We expect 33-inch all-terrain tires to complete the package.

2021 Ford Ranger Raptor Price


There is no official Ford Ranger Raptor price yet, but we expect it to be reasonable and shouldn't cost as much as the Ford F-150 Raptor,which starts around $54,000. We estimate that the new Ford Ranger Raptor will start around $42,000. It would probably cost more than the 2021 Ford Ranger Lariat,which starts at $32,910 and is the current top trim level. However, we expect it to be similarly priced to competitors, such as the Chevy Colorado ZR2 at $44,095 or the Toyota Tacoma TRD Pro, which starts at $45,080. Both the Chevy Colorado and Toyota Tacoma trucks are usually slightly higher priced than the Ford Ranger.


2021 Ford Ranger Raptor Engine Options & Performance Specs


While the European Ranger Raptor uses a diesel four-cylinder under the hood, we don't expect that to be the model we receive. We also don't expect to see a Ford Ranger Raptor V8 either. All signs point to a turbocharged2.3-liter four-cylinder, such as the one found in the Ford Focus RS or under the Mustang's hood. It's capable of producing up to 350 horsepower, plus 350pound-feet of torque. We expect it to be equipped with a ten-speed automatic transmission, just like what is found on other Ranger models.


Ford Ranger Raptor Towing Capacity & Payload Rating


The standard Ranger SuperCrew is capable of hauling up to1,860 pounds when equipped with a five-foot bed. Additionally, most Ranger models can tow up to 7,500 pounds with the right equipment. Much like the F-150Raptor, the Ranger Raptor will most likely only be available on larger cab configurations like the Ranger SuperCrew, so towing and payload power should remain similar. While we don't know how these numbers will line up with the Mini Ford Raptor exactly, we know that the truck will be capable. We will update the Ford Ranger Raptor specs as soon as they are available.


The 2021 Ford Ranger comes with seating to hold four or five occupants, depending on the configuration. Hopefully, the new Ford Ranger Raptor offers the same options. Additionally, the interior is high-end with standard features, including a Wi-Fi hot spot, Bluetooth, a four-speaker sound system, and a USB port. On higher-end Ranger trim models, possibly including the Raptor, you can also find Android Auto, Apple CarPlay, two USB ports, HD Radio, satellite radio, a ten-speaker Bang & Olufsen audio system, a navigation system, and an eight-inch touch screen. You can also opt for dual-zone automatic climate control, push-button start, and remote start.

2021 Ford Ranger Exterior Dimensions & Colors

The 2021 Ford Ranger dimensions measure 211″ L x 78″ W x71-72″ H, but we don't know where the Raptor model will fit in. We understand that the standard Ranger models use either 16-inch, 17-inch, or 18-inch wheels,but we hope to see something bigger to handle those off-road trails like the Raptor's 33-inch tires. At this time, we expect that the 2021 Ford Ranger Raptor length will fall right around the 211" of the original Ranger. We also assume that the 2021 Ford Ranger Raptor curb weight would fall between 3,922 and 4,441pounds, while the gross vehicle weight might be right around 6,050 pounds. While we don't know the available colors yet, we can hope that some of the four new options are available, such as Cyber Orange,Carbonized Gray, Cactus Gray, or Velocity Blue.
